COURSES

Center Based Learning

​Our on-site training courses are available to everyone, from those who are familiar with the treatments to those who have had no experience at all. All our courses are certified and each has a course manual along with other helpful tips and tools for you to take away with you. Our centre based courses are non-residential and held at our relaxing premises which is only 20 minutes outside of Glasgow.


Book yourself onto one of our courses and enjoy learning the benefits of these holistic treatments.

Home Study Learning

Unlike our centre based courses, you can take the time you need to work through the course material while having access to ask us any questions that you may have whilst you study. You also have lifetime access to the course materials so you can always refer back to your course material as and when you need to. ​


Purchase one of our on-line home study courses and enjoy learning the benefits of these holistic treatments.
